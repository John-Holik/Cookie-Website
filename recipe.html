Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Butter - 1 cup (2 sticks), softened
  • Granulated sugar - ¾ cup
  • Brown sugar - ¾ cup, packed
  • Eggs - 2 large
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  • All-purpose flour - 2 ¼ cups
  • Baking soda - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - ½ teaspoon
  • Chocolate chips - 2 cups (semi-sweet, or your preference)

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven
    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper or lightly grease them.
  2. Mix the Wet Ingredients
    In a large bowl, use a mixer to beat 1 cup of softened butter, ¾ cup of granulated sugar, and ¾ cup of brown sugar until the mixture is creamy and smooth (about 2-3 minutes). Add 2 eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition. Then mix in 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  3. Combine the Dry Ingredients
    In a separate bowl, whisk together 2 ¼ cups of all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon of baking soda, and ½ teaspoon of salt.
  4. Mix Wet and Dry Ingredients
    Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, beating on low speed until fully combined. The dough will be thick.
  5. Fold in Chocolate Chips
    Stir in 2 cups of chocolate chips (and 1 cup of nuts if using) with a spatula or wooden spoon until evenly distributed.
  6. Shape the Cookies
    Scoop tablespoon-sized portions of dough and drop them onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake
    Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers look slightly soft.
  8. Cool the Cookies
    Let the cookies cool on the baking sheets for about 2 minutes, then transfer them to wire racks to cool completely.
